# Literature Map

## Core ZNE
- Li & Benjamin (2017): Original ZNE proposal via Richardson extrapolation
- Temme, Bravyi, Gambetta (2017): Probabilistic error cancellation + extrapolation
- Giurgica-Tiron et al. (2020): Digital ZNE with unitary folding

## ZNE theory and limitations
- Cai (2021): Multi-exponential error extrapolation
- Takagi et al. (2022): Fundamental limits of QEM — exponential sampling overhead
- Quek et al. (2024): Exponentially tighter bounds on QEM limitations
- Tsubouchi et al. (2023): Universal cost of QEM

## Constrained / bounded approaches
- Miranskyy et al. (2026): Bounded ZNE with model selection (arXiv:2604.24475)
- Nguyen (2026): Physically-bounded ZNE + AICc (quantum-noise-optimizer)

## Inverse problems and regularization
- Hadamard (1902): Well-posedness of inverse problems
- Tikhonov (1963): Regularization of ill-posed problems
- Hansen (1998): Rank-deficient and discrete ill-posed problems

## Model selection
- Akaike (1974): AIC
- Hurvich & Tsai (1989): AICc for small samples
- Burnham & Anderson (2002): Model selection and multimodel inference

## Bayesian approaches to QEM
- Ferrie (2014): Bayesian quantum estimation
- O'Brien et al. (2021): Error mitigation via verified phase estimation (Bayesian flavor)

## Lower bounds and escape hatches
- Takagi et al. (2022): Generic exponential overhead
- Quek et al. (2024): Tighter bounds, structured exceptions noted
- Cai et al. (2023): QEM review — discusses when mitigation helps
